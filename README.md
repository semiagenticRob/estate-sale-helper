# Estate Sale Helper

Find estate sales near you. Browse photos, check hours, get directions, and save your favorites — all in one app.

Available on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6761272980).

---

## Support

Having trouble with the app? We're here to help.

- **Open a support ticket**: [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/semiagenticRob/estate-sale-helper/issues)
- **Email**: support@estatesalehelper.com

Please include your device model and iOS version when reporting issues.

---

## Features

- Search estate sales by city, zip code, or your current location
- Filter by distance (5–100 miles) and date range
- View results on an interactive map or scrollable list
- Browse sale photos, hours, and accepted payment methods
- Save sales to your favorites for quick access
- One-tap directions to any sale
- See sale status at a glance — active, upcoming, or ended
