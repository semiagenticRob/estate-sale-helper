## Description
Estate Helper makes it easy to find estate sales near you. Search by location, browse sale photos, check dates and hours, see accepted payment methods, and save sales you want to attend.

Whether you're a weekend treasure hunter, a reseller sourcing inventory, or just love a good estate sale — Estate Helper puts everything you need in one place.

FEATURES:
- Search by city, zip code, or your current location
- Filter by distance (5 to 100 miles) and date range
- View sales on a list or interactive map
- Browse sale photos, hours, and payment methods
- Save sales for quick access later
- Get directions with one tap
- See sale status at a glance — active, upcoming, or ended

Estate Helper is free to use. New sales are added daily across Colorado, with more states coming soon.
