Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Estate Sale Helper ("the App") helps you discover estate sales near you. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data the App accesses.

Location Data

The App requests your device location solely to show estate sales near you. Your location is sent to our search service to calculate distances and is not stored on our servers. You can deny location access at any time and manually enter a city or zip code instead.

Data We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account. Saved sales are stored locally on your device using on-device storage and are not transmitted to any server.

We do not collect personal information, analytics, advertising identifiers, or usage data.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — hosts the estate sale database. No personal data is sent.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — provides location search and geocoding. Queries include the city or address you type but no device identifiers.
  • EstateSales.net — original source of estate sale listing data. The App displays publicly available listing information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@estatesalehelper.com.
