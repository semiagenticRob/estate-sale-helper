## Description (4000 chars max)

**Estate Sale Helper** is the smarter way to find and plan estate sale runs — whether you're hunting for furniture, vintage items, collectibles, or hidden gems.

**FIND SALES NEAR YOU**
Search by location and distance. See full sale details including preview images, exact addresses, start and end times, payment methods accepted, and sale company info. Get everything you need before you leave home.

**PLAN YOUR ROUTE**
New in v2: Build a multi-stop route from your saved sales. Tap "Build Route" on your Saved screen and Estate Sale Helper maps the most efficient order — opening in Apple Maps or Google Maps so you can navigate turn-by-turn. Hit five sales in one Saturday morning without retracing your steps.

**SAVE THE ONES YOU WANT**
Star any sale to save it instantly. Your Saved list is always a tap away — see which ones are active, upcoming, or ended. Clear it when the weekend is over and start fresh.

**COMMUNITY RATINGS**
Rate sales you've attended. Estate Sale Helper uses your location to confirm attendance automatically — no check-in required. See aggregate ratings from other buyers before you waste a trip on an overpriced sale.

**AI IMAGE SEARCH** *(Pro)*
Looking for a specific piece? Search across every active sale in your area by what's in the photos — "mid-century lamp," "cast iron skillet," "vintage Pyrex." Estate Sale Helper Pro scans the images at sales near you so you can find it before someone else does.
